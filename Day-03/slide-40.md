### 2. Skills complementing MCP servers (Skills jo MCP servers ke saath mil kar kaam karti hain)
Yeh bohot important trend hai. **MCP (Model Context Protocol)** ek universal standard hai jo AI agents ko **real business data** se connect karta hai â€” jaise CRM, databases, APIs, file systems, etc.
