### **AI Agent**

AI Agent aik smart software hota hai jo **khud observe karta hai, decision leta hai, action karta hai aur seekhta rehta hai**. Ye human ke baghair kaam karta hai, bilkul aik digital worker ki tarah.

---

### **MCP (Model Context Protocol)**

MCP aik **universal rule / protocol** hai jiske zariye AI agent **tools, databases aur APIs** se connect hota hai. Matlab agent real duniya ke systems ke sath safely kaam kar sakta hai.

---

### **Spec-Driven Development (SDD)**

Is approach mein **pehle poori specification likhi jati hai**, phir AI us spec ke mutabiq code generate karta hai. Is se errors kam aur system zyada reliable hota hai.

---

### **SKILL.md**

SKILL.md aik **Markdown file** hoti hai jisme agent ko sikhaya jata hai ke **kis tarah kaam karna hai, logic kya hoga aur workflow kaise chalega**. Ye agent ki training manual hoti hai.

---

### **General Agent**

General Agent aik **flexible agent** hota hai jo har tarah ke kaam kar sakta hai (jaise Claude Code). Ye builder ke liye hota hai, production ke liye nahi.

---

### **Custom Agent**

Custom Agent aik **purpose-built agent** hota hai jo **specific kaam** ke liye banaya jata hai, guardrails ke sath. Ye **real production mein use hota hai**.

---

### **Agent Factory**

Agent Factory aik **system** hai jo **General Agents + Specs** use karke **bohat saare Custom Agents aur Skills automatically bana sakta hai**. Matlab agents ka factory system.

---

### **Digital FTE**

Digital FTE ka matlab AI agent ko **full-time employee** ki tarah sell karna. Ye **168 ghante haftay ke kaam karta hai**, jabke human sirf 40 ghante.

---

### **Shadow Mode**

Shadow Mode mein AI agent **human ke sath parallel kaam karta hai**, lekin decision implement nahi hota. Sirf **compare** kiya jata hai ke agent vs human ka result kya tha.

---

### **Human-in-the-Loop (HITL)**

Is model mein **important ya risky decisions** ke liye **human approval zaroori hota hai**. Agent akela final decision nahi leta.

---

### **Orchestration**

Orchestration ka matlab **multiple agents aur skills ko coordinate karna** taa ke aik complex task step-by-step complete ho sake.

---

### **Guardrails**

Guardrails **hard rules** hotay hain jo batatay hain ke agent **kya kar sakta hai aur kya nahi**. Ye production safety ke liye bohat zaroori hotay hain.
