## The Core Strategic Decision

(Yani Agentic AI automation ke liye do main paths)

> Jab hum AI agents design karte hain, do approach hoti hain: **General Agents** aur **Custom Agents**

---

### **Option A: The "Smart Consultant" (General Agents)**

**Key Points:**

* AI agents **flexible aur autonomous** hote hain
* **High-level reasoning** karte hain
* Complex tasks ko samajh kar **khud plan aur execute** karte hain

**Examples:**

* Claude Code
* Goose

**Analogy:**

> Ye bilkul **senior employee** jaisa hai jo problem samajh kar khud solution nikalta hai

**Focus:**

* Autonomy
* Problem-solving
* Flexible workflows

---

### **Option B: The "Assembly Line" (Custom Agents)**

**Key Points:**

* AI agents **specific task ke liye design** hote hain
* Repeatable workflows aur **process control** me strong hote hain
* Kam flexibility, lekin high reliability

**Examples:**

* OpenAI Agents SDK

**Analogy:**

> Ye bilkul **factory machine** jaisa hai jo har dafa perfect kaam karta hai

**Focus:**

* Reliability
* Process control
* Specific, repeatable tasks

---

### **Easy Way to Remember for Quiz**

| Type           | Analogy          | Focus                                         |
| -------------- | ---------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| General Agents | Smart Consultant | Autonomy + Flexibility + High-level reasoning |
| Custom Agents  | Assembly Line    | Reliability + Specific workflow               |

> **One-line mnemonic:**
> **“Consultant thinks, Machine repeats”**
