## AI Agent kya hota hai?

**AI Agent** aik aisa software hota hai jo

* **apna goal hasil karta hai**,
* **apne environment ko observe karta hai**,
* **decision leta hai ke next kya karna hai**,
* **actions perform karta hai** (jaise tools ya APIs use karna),
* aur **results se seekh kar** ye process bar-bar repeat karta rehta hai
  jab tak **goal complete na ho jaye**.

Yani agent sirf jawab nahi deta,
**kaam khud karta hai**.
