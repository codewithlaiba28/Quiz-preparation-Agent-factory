### Mukhtasar Tareef aur Maqsad
Ye book kehti hai ke agar aap apni expertise (jaise legal, sales, ya finance ka knowledge) ko AI agents mein daal dein, to wo aap ke liye paise kama sakte hain jab aap so rahe hon. Misal ke taur par, 2023 mein Casetext naam ki company Thomson Reuters ne $650 million mein khareedi, kyun ke un ka AI tool CoCounsel legal kaam kart tha jaise documents review aur research. Ye proof hai ke encoded expertise kitni qeemti hai.

Book ka vision hai ke aap AI agents banayein jo aap ki skills ko capture karein aur unko sell karein jaise digital full-time employees (FTEs). Ye living book hai, matlab is mein embedded presentation update hoti rehti hai taake latest info mile.

### Do Raaste Monetization Ke
Book do paths batati hai AI products banane ke:

1. **General Agents (Builder Path)**: Tools jaise Claude Code, Gemini CLI. Ye agents reasoning karte hain – problem dekhte hain, plan banate hain, code likhte hain, aur mistakes fix karte hain. Ye flexible hain, novel problems ke liye best. Aap sirf intent specify karte ho, baqi agent handle karta hai.

2. **Custom Agents (Product Path)**: Tools jaise OpenAI SDK. Ye specific tasks ke liye banaye jaate hain, reliable hote hain. Weeks lagte hain banane mein, lekin customer-ready hote hain.

Key insight: General agents custom agents banane mein madad karte hain. Aap spec likho, AI build kare, aur sell karo.

### Digital FTE Ki Value
Digital FTE human employee se behtar hai kyun ke:
- 24/7 available (168 hours/week vs 40).
- Sasta ( $500-2000/month vs $4000-8000).
- Instant deploy, consistent, aur asani se scale kar sakte ho.

Misal: Ek Digital SDR (Sales Development Rep) leads ko 2 minutes mein respond karta hai, 1000+ outreaches per day handle karta hai, jab ke human sirf 50 kar sakta hai. Result: Cost kam, efficiency zyada.

### Monetize Karne Ke Chaar Tareeqe
1. **Subscription**: Monthly fee for managed agent.
2. **Success Fee**: Commission on results.
3. **License**: Annual fee for agent logic.
4. **Marketplace**: OpenAI Apps jaise platforms par sell karo, jahan millions users hain.

### Required Skills
Ye blueprint hai, na ke shortcut. Aap ko skills seekhni hongi jaise:
- Specification writing (clear requirements).
- AI collaboration (teacher/student/co-worker dynamic).
- MCP (Model Context Protocol) for data integration.
- Testing (97% accuracy).
- Deployment (cloud-native).

Nine Pillars hain jaise AI CLI, Markdown as programming, etc.

### Book Ka Roadmap
Book phases mein divided hai:
- Foundation: Spec writing, setup.
- Products: Custom agents build.
- Revenue: Deployment aur selling.

Ye book domain experts, developers, aur entrepreneurs ke liye hai jo apna knowledge productize karna chahte hain.

### Paradigm Shift
Ab coding se shift ho kar orchestrating par – aap conductor ho, AI orchestra. Specs living contracts hain jo AI execute karta hai.

Aakhir mein, ye book practical hai, theory nahi. Aap seekh kar real Digital FTEs bana sakte ho jo business banayein.
