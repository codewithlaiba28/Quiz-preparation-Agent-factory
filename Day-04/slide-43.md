Yeh picture **"Moving up the stack"** ko explain kar rahi hai - yani technology stack mein upar ki taraf ja rahe hain:

**Teen Layers hain (andar se bahar):**

1. **Models (Processors)** - Sabse andar, orange circle
   - Yeh basic AI models hain jaise Claude
   - Yeh "processors" ki tarah kaam karte hain - raw computing power

2. **Agents (Operating Systems)** - Beech mein, grey circle
   - Models ke upar agent layer hai
   - Yeh "operating systems" ki tarah hain
   - Agents models ko use karke autonomous decisions lete hain aur tasks perform karte hain

3. **Skills (Applications)** - Sabse bahar, beige circle
   - Yeh end-user applications hain
   - Skills agents ko specific capabilities dete hain
   - Jaise phone mein apps hote hain, waise hi

**Concept:**
Pehle sirf models thay (ChatGPT jaise). Phir agents aaye jo models ko orchestrate karte hain. Ab skills aa rahe hain jo agents ko practical, real-world tasks ke liye equip karti hain.
