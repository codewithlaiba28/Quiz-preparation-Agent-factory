### **AI Implementation ke Liye Team Structure**

Book kehta hai ke **AI agents ka successful deployment sirf technology se nahi hota, balki team aur process pe depend karta hai.** Har stage ke liye alag roles aur size zaroori hain.

---

### **1. CORE TEAM (Required)**

* Ye wo log hain jo **har AI project ke liye mandatory** hain, chhoti teams se start karo:

**POC / Pilot Stage:**

* **AI Product Owner:**

  * Roadmap own karta hai
  * Use cases prioritize karta hai
  * Success metrics define karta hai
* **Security / Compliance Lead:**

  * Audits aur access controls manage karta hai
  * Regulatory alignment ensure karta hai
* **Team Size:** 2-3 log

---

**Production Stage:**

* **Agent Engineer:**

  * AI agent ke skills, prompts aur workflows build karta hai
  * Multi-channel platforms (MCP) integrate karta hai
* **MLOps Engineer:**

  * Monitoring aur deployment pipelines manage karta hai
  * Cost optimization aur performance ensure karta hai
* **Team Size:** 4-6 log

---

### **2. EXTENDED TEAM (Scale)**

* Ye log tab add hote hain jab **enterprise level ya scale** chahiye:

**Enterprise Scale Stage:**

* **Domain Expert:**

  * Business logic provide karta hai
  * AI outputs validate karta hai
  * Edge cases train karta hai
* **Change Manager:**

  * Training aur adoption track karta hai
  * Resistance manage karta hai
* **Team Size:** 8-12 log

---

### **Rule of Thumb (Book ke mutabiq):**

* **1 Agent Engineer per 3-5 production agents**
* Hamesha **chhoti core team se start karo**, phir scale karo
