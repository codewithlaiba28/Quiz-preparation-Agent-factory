### **AI Agent Platforms ke Liye Competitive Landscape (2026)**

Book kehti hai ke **enterprise deployment me platform selection strategic decision hai**, sirf technical features dekhna kaafi nahi.

---

### **1. Recommendation (Book ke mutabiq)**

* **Development Phase:**

  * **Claude Code + Spec Kit Plus** use karo.
  * Ye tools development ke liye flexible aur rapid prototyping allow karte hain.
* **Production Phase:**

  * **OpenAI / Claude Agent SDK** recommended hai.
  * Ye enterprise-grade deployment, scaling aur integration ke liye reliable hai.

**Book ka principle:** Development aur production ke liye **alags tools aur SDKs use karo**, jisse testing aur scaling smooth ho.

---

### **2. 2026 Trend (Book ke mutabiq)**

* **MCP (Multi-Channel Platform) adoption** ab table stakes ban gaya hai:

  * Har platform multiple channels aur touchpoints support karta hai.
* **Skills standardization across platforms:**

  * AI agent development aur deployment me workflows, prompts, aur skill sets ab standardized ho rahe hain.
  * Ye ensure karta hai ke teams ek platform se doosre platform pe smoothly switch kar saken.

**Book ka principle:** Market me competition aur trends ko samajhna zaroori hai. Jo platform modern features aur standardization provide kare, wo long-term value dega.
