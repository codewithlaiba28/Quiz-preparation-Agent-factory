### **Top 6 AI Agent Deployment Failures aur Unko Avoid Karna**

---

#### 1. **Over-Automating Too Fast**

* Book kehta hai ke bohot jaldi sab processes automate karna risky hai. Agar AI har cheez mein turant use ho jaye to galti ka risk zyada hota hai.
* **Fix (Book ke mutabiq):** Pehle 1-2 **low-risk processes** se start karo. Pehle value prove karo, phir scale karo.

---

#### 2. **Ignoring Edge Cases**

* AI aksar unusual scenarios ya **exceptions** ko handle nahi kar pati. Agar edge cases ignore kiye jayein to system fail ho sakta hai.
* **Fix:** Sab exceptions document karo aur **escalation paths** define karo har decision branch ke liye.

---

#### 3. **No Monitoring or Alerting**

* Book kehta hai ke AI ko deploy karte hi monitoring aur alerts enable karna zaroori hai. Agar performance track na ho to errors detect nahi ho sakte.
* **Fix:** Day 1 se **observability** implement karo. Track karo:

  * Accuracy
  * Latency
  * Cost per task

---

#### 4. **Vendor Lock-In**

* Agar AI system kisi ek vendor ya platform pe tightly depend ho jaye, to future changes aur migration mushkil ho jati hai.
* **Fix:** **Abstraction layers** build karo jisse portability ho. Panaversity is concept par kaam kar rahi hai.

---

#### 5. **Underestimating Change Management**

* AI adoption sirf technical problem nahi hai, organization aur logon ke liye bhi challenge hai. Agar teams ready na hon, to adoption fail ho sakta hai.
* **Fix:** Affected teams ko **early train** karo. AI ko replacement nahi, **augmentation** ke taur pe position karo.

---

#### 6. **No Success Metrics Defined**

* Agar clear KPIs define na hon to success measure nahi ho sakti aur galti ka pata nahi chalega.
* **Fix:** Pehle **KPIs define** karo, jaise:

  * Time saved
  * Error rate
  * Cost
  * User satisfaction

---

### **Core Principle from Book**

* **80% AI agent failures organizational issues ki wajah se hoti hain, technical issues ki wajah se nahi.**
* Pehle **people aur process** plan karo, phir technology deploy karo.
