### **AI Agent Deployment: Budget Planning aur Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)**

Book kehti hai ke **AI deployment ka success sirf technical setup pe nahi, balki cost planning aur ROI par bhi depend karta hai.** Har scale ke liye cost alag hoti hai, aur token-based pricing ko samajhna zaroori hai.

---

### **1. Monthly Cost by Scale**

**Starter (Small Pilot / POC):**

* **Cost:** $500–2K per month
* **Scope:** 1-2 agents, 10K tasks/month

**Growth Stage:**

* **Cost:** $5–15K per month
* **Scope:** 5-10 agents, 100K tasks/month

**Enterprise Stage:**

* **Cost:** $30–100K per month
* **Scope:** 20+ agents, 1M+ tasks/month

**Book ka principle:** Scale ke saath cost aur complexity dono increase hoti hain. Pehle small pilot se start karo, ROI validate karo, phir scale karo.

---

### **2. Token Costs (December 2025)**

* **GPT-5.2:** $1.75 per 400k input tokens • $14 per 128k output tokens
* **Gemini 3 Flash Preview:** $0.5 per 1M input • $3 per M output
* **DeepSeek-V3.2:** $0.28 per M input • $0.42 per 1M output
* **Claude Opus 4.5:** $5 per 1M input • $25 per M output

**Book ka principle:**

* Token costs differ per model, aur deployment budget me ye **variable cost** ka major part hota hai.
* Model selection aur optimization se token costs kaafi reduce ki ja sakti hain.

---

### **3. ROI Formula**

**Formula (Book ke mutabiq):**
[
\text{Savings} = \text{Hours Saved} \times \text{Rate}
]

* Target payback period: **3–6 months**
* Book kehti hai ke deployment ka ROI **continuous measurement** chahiye, aur savings ko track karna zaroori hai.

---

### **4. Cost Trends & Planning**

* Token costs **30–50% annually down trend** kar rahe hain
* Model upgrades **har 6–12 months** plan karne chahiye
* Book ka principle: **Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) ko proactive manage karo**, na ke reactive.

---

✅ **Summary :**

* Deployment scale determine karta hai monthly cost
* Token costs model aur usage pe depend karte hain
* ROI ko measure karo aur payback target rakho
* Cost optimization aur model upgrades plan karna zaroori hai
