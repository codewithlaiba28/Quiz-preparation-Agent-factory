## **Main Difference (Exam Focus)**

### **Early Coding Agents**

* Prediction-centric hotay hain
* Next token / line guess karte hain
* Errors par stuck ho jate hain

### **General Agents**

* Reasoning-centric hotay hain
* Sochte hain, act karte hain, result check karte hain
* **Khud se correction** karte hain

---

## **Why ye important hai?**

Isi reasoning loop ki wajah se:

* General agents **complex tasks** handle kar pate hain
* Tumhein **har step pe hand-holding** nahi karni parti
* Ye agents **real digital employees** jaise behave karte hain
