## **Slide ka Core Message (Book Style)**

### **Coding Agents**

* Software-native hotay hain
* Development workflows ke liye optimized
* Authority limited hoti hai

### **General Agents**

* **Goal-native** hotay hain
* Unhein **broader objectives** par trust diya jata hai
* Tools khud choose karte hain
* Domains cross kar sakte hain
* **Code ko universal interface** bana kar system level par act karte hain
