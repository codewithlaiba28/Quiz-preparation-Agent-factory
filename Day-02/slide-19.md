## **Best For (Konse kaam ke liye best hai)**

### **Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)**

Jab task bohat **clear rules aur steps** ke sath hota hai, tab custom agent perfect hota hai ‚Äî jis tarah enterprises SOPs define karte hain. 

---

### **High-volume continuous tasks**

Jaise **5,000 invoices process karna** ‚Äî repetitive ya batch work ‚Äî ye tasks automated aur specialized workflows me zyada efficient hote hain. 

---

### **Customer-facing interactions (strict safety)**

Jahan **strict safety, rules aur policies** follow karna zaroori ho (e.g., return policies, compliance), tab custom agents ek ‚Äúarchitecture + controlled AI component‚Äù model best rehta hai.
